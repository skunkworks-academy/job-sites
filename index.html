How to write better searches

Prompt checklist

  • Role: sales, support, developer, analyst, instructor, marketer.
  • Level: internship, entry-level, junior, senior, freelance, contract.
  • Location: South Africa, remote, international, hybrid, on-site.
  • Skills: cloud, AI, cybersecurity, sales, product, media, Microsoft.
  • Application readiness: keep a focused CV, portfolio and short cover note ready.
